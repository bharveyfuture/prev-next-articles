Which Xbox Games With Gold games are free in November?

By GamesRadar Staff

Shares

Games With Gold is going back in time in November, with Super Dungeon Bros and Murdered: Soul Suspect charging forth on Xbox One, while The Secret of Monkey Island and Far Cry 3: Blood Dragon give us a heavy dose of nostalgia on Xbox 360.

Remember: any games you choose to download during their availability period will remain accessible so long as you have an active Xbox Live Gold membership. And don't forget too that any Xbox 360 games made available through Games With Gold are also backwards compatible with Xbox One.

November 2016 free Games With Gold games

Super Dungeon Bros (Xbox One) - Available: November 1 - 30 Mashing together-as-nails dungeon-crawling, heavy-metal rock, co-op play, and a cartoonish style, Super Dungeon Bros aims to make smashing the undead and monsters as fun as it can be. Just watch out for the giant blades of death and booby traps.

Murdered: Soul Suspect (Xbox One) - Available: November 16 - December 15 Kudos to whoever came up with such a strong pun for this murder-mystery game about a detective in Salem who must combine his modern-day skills with knowledge given to him by ghosts of the past in order to catch a serial killer on the loose. There's just one catch to all of this: you're dead. Good luck!

The Secret of Monkey Island: Special Edition (Xbox 360) - Available: November 1 - 15 The classic point-and-click adventure game from visionary game creators Tim Schafer and Ron Gilbert is back and looking sharper than ever. Help Guybrush Threepwood fulfill his dream of becoming a pirate king by facing down the villainous LeChuck and his undead crew.

Far Cry 3: Blood Dragon (Xbox 360) - Available: November 16 - 30 Stuffed to the gills with '80s-inspired visuals and references, Far Cry 3: Blood Dragon is a glorious return to the hyper-macho, neon-tinted era when men were cyborgs, one-line quips made every hero clever, and training montages ruled. Be kind, rewind.

October 2016 free Games With Gold games 

  • Super Mega Baseball: Extra Innings (Xbox One)
  • The Escapists (Xbox One)
  • MX vs ATV Reflex (Xbox 360)
  • I Am Alive (Xbox 360)
